Welcome to COMP 466 Tutor!

COMP 466 Tutor is an application that allows users to learn about some of the web technologies that are covered in Unit 1, Unit 2 and Unit 3 of COMP 466. For each unit, a page is be provided that contains the summarized notes of the materials that is covered in the unit. Then, there will be a follow-up quiz that will assess your knowledge about the current unit you are on. Click Next to proceed.
